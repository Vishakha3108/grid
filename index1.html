Healthy Food

Healthy food refers to food that contains the right amount of nutrients to keep our body fit.There are many source of getting food. One of popular source is plant based. Plant based diets encompass a wide range of dietary patterns that contain high amount of nutrition. Plant products such as vegetables, fruits, whole grains, legumes, nuts and seeds.Appropriately planned vegetarian diets are healthful and nutritionally adequate for all stage of the human life cycle, including during pregnancy, lactation, infancy, childhood, and adolescence.
